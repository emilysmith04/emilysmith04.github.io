Research Experience:


Isolating B. Tequilensis from Soil Roots:


In this project, soil from the Sevilleta LTER was collected specifically around L. tridentada roots. Cultures were obtained with serial dilutions and then maintained by through weekly innoculation on nitrogen free culture plates. DNA extraction/PCR testing was done to verify the species.

Stable Isotope Analysis of C3 and C4 Plants:


In this project, nutrient utilization of nitrogen and carbon in L. tridentata (C3) and B. Eriopoda was done through Stable Isotope Analysis (SIA). Plants were grown in a greenhouse, homogenized with liquid nitrogen & a mortar/pestle, tins were encapsulated, and analyzed with a mass spectrometer.

Assistance in Isolate Fungi Growth Plates:

Fungi isolates obtained from the Sevilleta LTER were plugged and innoculated on potato starch culture plates containing differing amounts of salinity. Each isolate plate was duplicated such that different temperatures could be tested to measure growth rates. J imaging software was used to analyzed total growth over the period of 14 days.
